NAME
       dds - A safer and simpler way to use dd

SYNOPSIS
       dds

DESCRIPTION
       dds  is a high-level posix shell wrapper around dd. It focuses
       on serving the most common use cases with simplicity,  safety,
       and portability in mind.

OPTIONS
       dds  does  not take any options. However, --help and --version
       work as expected.
